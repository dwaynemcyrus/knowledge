Exploring their creativity and use artistic outlets as a means of self-expression, emotional release, and personal growth. 
## Why It’s Beneficial
- **Emotional Outlet:** Provides a healthy outlet for expressing and processing emotions, which is crucial for trauma survivors.
- **Self-Discovery:** Helps individuals discover and explore their identities and passions.
- **Stress Relief:** Engaging in creative activities can reduce stress and promote mental well-being.
- **Communication:** Enhances the ability to communicate feelings and thoughts non-verbally, which can be particularly helpful in relationship-building and healing processes.
