## Comparisons
**Selected Sales Pages**
- [Email Client Horde Sales Page](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1-bdXXC2hd64xvaAmwauTNURstq7lFobG/view?usp=share_link)
- [10X Landing Pages Sales Page](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1LSKEXnG208BuOwkp9kio6-XkMq4uI1S2/view?usp=share_link) 
- [Conversation Conversions - Sales Page](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1nmtOU2i1KwyZuJecfEPPmFGT2hXa0vJT/view?usp=share_link)
### What do they do differently? 
Conversation Conversions
- They broke the offer down into components and priced each
- Educated the reader deeply by addressing problems with quick solutions 
- Made use of video testimonials 
- Positioned himself as the one to learn from, “don’t make my mistakes”

10X Landing Pages Sales Page
- Paid attention to design and actually set their type
- Included various visual ques to encourage, delight, and pace the reading experience
- Lots of testimonial for social proof with names and faces

Email Client Horde
- They give no reassurance to the reader, it’s an I’m right take it or leave it approach
- 90% fascination bullets, little rapport building, plenty “authority” preaching 
- They are speaking to a very specific sleazy type of reader
### What do they do well?  
Conversation Conversions
- The header was presented like a news article and then immediately jumps into a deep uncovering  story
- Spent the intro connecting with the readers pain
- Reinforced social proof with “39 beta testers”
- Describe the dream state
- Reinforcing the decision the reader has to make by describing them with pain and scarcity/urgency for the one that purchases
- Made use of the bottom page to summarize and reinforce social proof with testimonials for those who skimmed down to the end instead of reading

10X Landing Pages Sales Page
- There's such a strong voice a though the writer is right infront of me, it's incredibly relatable like a friend who lives like me
- The addition of various testimonials throughout really help reinforce the point being made
- The page was intertwined with design which is made it pleasing and encouraging to continue reading with its visual transitions
- The walk through of various aspects of the product with visual examples highlighting various pain and desires with great precision
- Great use of the visual que on page 17 to contact, subtle messaging
- The pacing and the pull, I just couldn't stop reading more, I felt compelled to read more
- I was hooked within the first 2-4 pages, I literrlly would give her my money right now if I could 
- Emphasized the pains in a caring way with a mix of desires

Email Client Horde
- The fascination bullets are pretty good but over used
### What could you change to improve the effectiveness of the sales page?  
Conversation Conversions
- The layout, using design to improve the visual flow and more emphasis to key areas
- I wouldn’t mention to extra products I would leave them as Easter eggs after the purchase as a talking piece

10X Landing Pages Sales Page
- I don't think there's anything to change, I beleive my only consideration would be figuring out how to make this masterpiece 50% shorter to read. But than again I read the whole thing, so I don't that's really a problem.

Email Client Horde
- Layout the page with design so make the reading experience easy
- There's spelling mistakes so I would do a grammar check
- Half the amount of fascination bullets used in each section
