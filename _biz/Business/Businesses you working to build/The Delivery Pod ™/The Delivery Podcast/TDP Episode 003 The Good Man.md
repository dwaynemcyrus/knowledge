### Qualities of a Good Man
#### Confident
- A man of self-assurance, even if he uncertain of how to get something done, he knows he will figure it out
- Holds to his decisions and doesn’t waiver easily

#### Intelligent
- A great ability to acquires and applies knowledge
- He is intentional with his actions and non-reactive

#### Assertive
- Knows what he wants and works to get it
- There are expectations and standards to meet for his wife and those around him

#### Integrity
- Honest, trustworthy, and has strong moral values which typically align with what we now call “traditional”
- A man who strives to keep his word and follows through on his promises

#### Commitment 
- Puts in the time and effort needed to make his relationships work
- Don’t abandon his commitments because times got challenging

#### Responsible
- Understands that everything ends with him and lives in accordance
- Acknowledges his wrongs and makes efforts to correct any damage done

#### Communication
- Listens to the opinions of his wife and others to receive feedback 
- Means what he says, says what he means with consideration for those listening 

#### Respect 
- Respects his wife, his family, and those her interacts with 
- Makes considerations when needed

#### Patience
- Takes time to understand and hear his wife and others out
- Doesn’t rush anyone unless there is a pragmatic need for it 

#### Realistic
- He pays attention to what’s happening in the world around him
- Does not allow those he cares about to live in delusion

#### Professional
- Everything he does and accepts is with intent and purpose, he takes being a man seriously
- The way in which he presents himself to the world
