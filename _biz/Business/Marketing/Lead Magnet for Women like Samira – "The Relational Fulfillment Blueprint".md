**Lead Magnet for Women like Samira: "The Relational Fulfillment Blueprint"**

  

**Title:**

"The Relational Fulfillment Blueprint: 5 Steps to Heal from Past Trauma and Build Meaningful Relationships"

  

**Description:**

A free, comprehensive eBook designed specifically for women who have experienced trauma from dating unfit men. This blueprint provides actionable steps to begin the healing process and start building the fulfilling relationships you deserve. 

  

**Content Highlights:**

  

1. **Introduction:**

   - Brief introduction about the struggles women face after traumatic dating experiences.

   - Overview of what the eBook will cover and the transformation it promises.

  

2. **Step 1: Acknowledging and Understanding Your Trauma**

   - Explanation of how past experiences shape current relationship patterns.

   - Guided exercises for self-reflection and journaling prompts to understand emotional triggers.

  

3. **Step 2: Setting Boundaries and Standards**

   - Tips on how to establish clear boundaries in future relationships.

   - Worksheets for defining personal values and relationship standards.

  

4. **Step 3: Building Self-Worth and Confidence**

   - Daily affirmations and activities to boost self-esteem.

   - Practical advice on self-care and personal growth.

  

5. **Step 4: Developing Emotional Intelligence**

   - Techniques for improving emotional awareness and empathy.

   - Exercises to practice active listening and emotional regulation.

  

6. **Step 5: Seeking Support and Creating a Support Network**

   - Guidance on finding the right support system, including friends, family, and professionals.

   - Tips on how to join support groups and online communities for shared experiences and advice.

  

**Bonus Material:**

   - "30-Day Relationship Reflection Journal" - A digital journal to track progress and reflect on personal growth.

   - "Self-Care Checklist" - A printable checklist with self-care activities and tips.

  

**Call to Action:**

   - Encourage readers to share the eBook with friends who might benefit from it.

   - Provide a link to sign up for a free 7-day email course that delves deeper into each step of the blueprint.

   - Offer an invitation to join a private, supportive online community where they can connect with others on a similar journey.

  

**Benefits:**

   - Immediate access to practical tools and exercises to start the healing process.

   - A sense of empowerment from taking the first steps towards building healthier relationships.

   - A supportive community and ongoing resources to aid in personal growth and relational fulfillment.

  

**How to Promote:**

   - Use compelling social media ads targeting women who have experienced dating trauma.

   - Share testimonials and success stories from women who have used the blueprint.

   - Offer the eBook as an exclusive freebie for email subscribers on your website.

  

This lead magnet is designed to be highly valuable, providing immediate benefits and actionable steps, making it compelling for women like Samira to not only sign up but also share it with their friends.
