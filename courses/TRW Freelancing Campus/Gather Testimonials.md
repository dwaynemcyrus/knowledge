### Fundamentals of Closing via Phone
- Skip the small talk in the call, you are busy and they are busy, get straight to the point by asking a question to get the ball rolling “Thank them for being on the call recognize they are busy and ask what motivated them to get on the call”
- You want to talk less and as little as possible, the purpose of the sales call is to diagnose their problem to learn whether you can solve it and in turn if they are a good client for you
- If a person isn’t a good fit do not take their money, “ where you are in your business is not a good fit right now if you do get to xyz please feel free to reach out. If you know of anyone who you could refer me to I would greatly appreciate it”
- You need to think in your feet and listen to what their main issue is that they have instead of just pushing what you want, you are a doctor of branding, marketing, and sales. 
- Get to the point, you don’t need to 20+ minutes for a sales call, 10-15 minutes is more than enough. A 20+ minute call is for actual client strategy sessions
### How to Onboard Clients Smoothly
- Getting all of the social proof
- Access to all their products for review
- Links to all their sales and opt-in pages 
- Have a clear contract that is direct and outlining the expectations 
- It’s best to let the client provide all of the required documents and in each folder give them a clear document outlining what is needed
- You want to get them paid quickly afterwards, so get to work, show them your results! 
### What to Do About Contracts

- Make use of the contract templates provided
- Consider making a guarantee that you will work with them until they are satisfied more on this in another module 
- You NEED a contract
- Make use of DocuSign, DocHub, Lumin PDF
## How to Communicate with Your Clients
- Never allow people from your team be the main contact with your client
- In communication you want to have moments of personal sharing 
- On Saturday or Sunday give the clients an update on the past week and what to expect in the coming week
- Send updates when certain aspects of work are done
- Keep your clients updated regularly you are a team so every 1-2 days is great 
- The teacher sometimes goes into the clients Stripe accounts to check sales and update them on whether it slow or not and a small snippet on how we’ll improve or maintain
