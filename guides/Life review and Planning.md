## Weekly Review & Planning
Prepare a blank scratchpad for quick notes on paper or digitally with the iPad, digital preferred.

Read the [[Rules for Capturing]] (this can be found [here](yearly-review.md)) and review the [Processing Workflow Diagram]() 

Capture all of the loose physical items into your physical inbox

Capture all of the loose digital items into your digital inbox

Capture all of the loose thoughts using the [Trigger Lists]() and place those items into your task manager inbox

Capture all necessary details from your daily review & planning logs

Capture all necessary details from your various journal entries

Read the [[Rules for Processing]] (this can be found [here](yearly-review.md)) and review the [Processing Workflow Diagram]() 

Process all of your physical items placed in your physical inbox

Process all of your digital items placed in your digital inbox

Process all of your communication platforms to zero

Debrief: Report or log all of your life stats and fill out quick summaries
