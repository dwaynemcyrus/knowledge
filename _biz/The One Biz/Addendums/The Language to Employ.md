##### Deeper Meaning
1. **Cultivate the Mind**: This phase signifies the importance of nurturing and developing a mindful, growth-oriented mindset. It suggests an active, intentional process of preparing oneself for growth and healing, resonating with the idea that change begins with the mind's cultivation.
2. **Heal the Wound**: Directly addressing the aspect of healing, this phrase acknowledges the presence of wounds—be they emotional, psychological, or spiritual—and emphasizes the proactive stance towards healing them. It speaks to the heart of your work, recognizing the pain and challenges your clients face and offering a promise of healing.
3. **Extract the Wisdom**: The concluding part of the motto highlights the transformative potential of the healing journey. It suggests that from the process of healing, valuable insights and wisdom are gained. This phase underscores the idea that experiences, even painful ones, hold lessons that contribute to one’s personal and spiritual growth.

#### Peace to Power
The phrase “peace to power” can have a few interpretations depending on context:

	1.	Inner Transformation: It could signify the journey from inner peace or tranquility to personal empowerment and strength. This journey often involves overcoming obstacles, gaining self-confidence, and harnessing one’s inner resources to achieve goals.
	2.	External Influence: It might also represent the transition from a state of peace or calm to taking action and making an impact in the world. This could apply to personal growth, leadership, or advocacy where individuals move from passive observation to active engagement and influence.
	3.	Philosophical or Spiritual Growth: In a deeper sense, “peace to power” could reflect spiritual or philosophical growth, where inner peace leads to a sense of personal power or enlightenment, enabling individuals to make positive changes in their lives and communities.

Overall, “peace to power” encapsulates a journey of transformation and empowerment, whether on a personal, social, or spiritual level, emphasizing the evolution from a state of peace to one of strength and influence.
