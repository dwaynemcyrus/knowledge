### What Is The Genius Method? 
- The Genius Method is a way of transforming boring ideas into Genius Ideas.
- An all-in-one process for note-taking, journaling, and generating original ideas for articles, newsletters, videos, tweets, posts, speeches, and more.

- You will take good ideas from what you consume, see online, or have in your head
