### 8-Week Core Program Framework

**Objective**: To guide trauma survivors through a transformative journey, helping them reclaim their self-worth, set healthy boundaries, build emotional intelligence, and establish fulfilling relationships.

#### Week 1: Introduction and Setting Intentions
**Objective**: Establish a foundation of trust, outline program goals, and set personal intentions.

- **Session Content**:
  - Welcome and Program Overview
  - Icebreaker Activities to Build Group Cohesion
  - Setting Personal Goals and Intentions for the Program
  - Introduction to Trauma-Informed Care Principles
- **Activities**:
  - Journaling Exercise: “My Intentions and Hopes for This Journey”
  - Group Discussion: Sharing Personal Goals
- **Homework**:
  - Reflective Journaling on Past Experiences and Future Aspirations

#### Week 2: Understanding and Healing Trauma
**Objective**: Help participants recognize the impact of trauma and begin the healing process.

- **Session Content**:
  - Understanding Trauma: Definitions, Types, and Effects
  - The Science of Trauma: How It Affects the Brain and Body
  - Introduction to Healing Modalities (e.g., mindfulness, somatic experiencing)
- **Activities**:
  - Guided Meditation for Grounding
  - Group Sharing: Personal Stories of Trauma
- **Homework**:
  - Mindfulness Practice: Daily Meditation or Breathing Exercises

#### Week 3: Identifying and Setting Boundaries
**Objective**: Teach participants how to identify their needs and establish healthy boundaries.

- **Session Content**:
  - The Importance of Boundaries in Healthy Relationships
  - How to Identify and Communicate Your Needs
  - Practical Strategies for Setting and Maintaining Boundaries
- **Activities**:
  - Role-Playing Scenarios: Practicing Boundary-Setting
  - Boundary Mapping Exercise
- **Homework**:
  - Boundary Journal: Documenting Daily Boundary-Setting Experiences

#### Week 4: Building Self-Worth and Confidence
**Objective**: Empower participants to recognize their value and build self-confidence.

- **Session Content**:
  - Understanding Self-Worth: Self-Esteem vs. Self-Compassion
  - Techniques for Building Confidence
  - Overcoming Self-Doubt and Negative Self-Talk
- **Activities**:
  - Self-Affirmation Exercises
  - Visualization Practice: Imagining Your Most Confident Self
- **Homework**:
  - Daily Self-Affirmations and Confidence-Building Activities

#### Week 5: Enhancing Emotional Intelligence
**Objective**: Develop participants’ emotional intelligence for better self-awareness and relationship management.

- **Session Content**:
  - What is Emotional Intelligence (EQ) and Why It Matters
  - Components of EQ: Self-Awareness, Self-Regulation, Motivation, Empathy, and Social Skills
  - Strategies for Improving EQ
- **Activities**:
  - Emotional Check-In: Identifying and Naming Emotions
  - Empathy Practice: Active Listening and Perspective-Taking
- **Homework**:
  - Emotional Journal: Tracking Emotions and Responses Daily

#### Week 6: Modern Dating and Healthy Relationships
**Objective**: Prepare participants for modern dating and building healthy relationships.

- **Session Content**:
  - Understanding Modern Dating Dynamics
  - Recognizing Red Flags and Green Flags in Relationships
  - Tips for Navigating Online Dating
- **Activities**:

  - Group Discussion: Sharing Dating Experiences and Lessons Learned
- **Homework**:
  - Reflective Exercise: Defining Your Ideal Relationship

#### Week 7: Embracing Vulnerabilities and Building Trust
**Objective**: Teach participants how to embrace vulnerability and build trust in relationships.

- **Session Content**:
  - The Power of Vulnerability in Authentic Connections
  - Building Trust: Foundations and Practices

- **Activities**:
  - Trust-Building Exercises

- **Homework**:

  - Action Planning Workshop: Creating a Roadmap for the Future

  - Final Reflection: Writing a Letter to Your Future Self
