Art Advisor
Full-time Position  
$72,000$83,000 20% Commission on Sales)

We are a leading art consultancy firm that specializes in providing bespoke art advisory services to high-net-worth individuals, emerging collectors, museums, and corporate clients globally. Our firm is dedicated to helping our clients build world-class art collections by providing expert advice on buying, selling, and managing art.

## Responsibilities

Work closely with clients to understand their art-collecting goals and objectives. Develop and implement strategic plans to build and manage art collections.

Conduct in-depth research on artists, art movements, and market trends to identify investment opportunities.  
Sourcing artworks for acquisition, including negotiating prices, managing transactions, and overseeing shipping and installation.

Develop and maintain relationships with collectors, dealers, galleries, auction houses, and other art world professionals.  
Prepare detailed reports and analyses on artworks and collections, including market trends and valuations.

Advise clients on pricing strategy, fair market value, collection management, including conservation, storage, and insurance.

Attend art fairs, exhibitions, and other art world events to stay up-to-date on market developments and opportunities.
