## Notes
### How to Pick Your Niche
- A niche is where your leads are at
- Start with the areas in which you are already interested in
- Start with the content you already consume
- You don’t need to stick with any niche
- Areas I’m already interested in: Personal Development, Finance, Art, Relationships, Psychology
### The Difference Between a Skill and a Niche
- I skill is a service you can offer someone 
- The niche is the areas of a market you are servicing
- You get paid for making them money and saving them time
