The host has niched into personal development with coaches, entrepreneurs, etc.

When selecting your niches, write for what you are excited about

Don't get caught up on selecting the right niche, work in a few to find out what works

The Profitable List of Niches 
- Online Education: website, courses, webinars, etc
- Software as a Service
- FinTech: financial transactions and management
- Sexual Wellness: a growing area of education in relation to tools, knowledge, activities, etc
- Healthy Snacks: a movement in eating healthier
- Pets: These are the new relationships and care for them is increasing with products to service
- Sustainable Products
- Lifestyle Subscription Boxes
- Survival: Hype from the pandemic and lockdowns increased an already growing market
