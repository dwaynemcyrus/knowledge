# Space Cleaning routine
## Daily Evening Reset
1. Put on your [[Mindset Audio|reprogramming audio]].
2. Food prep your meals for the next day and/or pack your food for tomorrow.
3. Clean the kitchen, dishes, and everything in the kitchen.
4. Organize the living room devices, seating, and upholstery with the floor and hand vacuum along with natural/homemade disinfectant spray.
5. Put away any left-out materials in your studio and cover any works (paintings, sculptures, etc.) in progress.
6. Wipe down and reorganize all of your workspaces, desks, pinboards, blackboards, etc. in the office as preparations for tomorrow.
7. Check the bathroom to ensure its in a clean condition, give the counters, sink, and toilet a disinfectant wipe.
8. Make sure the floor cleaning robot is prepared to vacuum and mop tonight.
10. Pack any and all materials needed for tomorrow's travels and layout your clothing.
11. In each room setup some incense or candles that can burn over night with a limit
12. Update all of your manual alarms for Salat on your phone
13. Place all of your devices to charge and backup locally and externally.

# Taqwa Routine and Guidance

***
## Forgiveness from Allah
1. Read the sins of the day and then make [[Tawbah]]
2. Thank Allah for another day with gratitude for what you experienced that day in conversation. and my [evening dua for protection](app://obsidian.md/dua).
3. Reciting your [[Al-Fatihah]] [Al-Fatihah](app://obsidian.md/al-fatihah) [Al-Ikhlas](app://obsidian.md/al-ikhlas) , [Al-Falaq](app://obsidian.md/al-falaq), [An-Nas](app://obsidian.md/an-nas), [Ayatul Kursi](app://obsidian.md/ayatul-kursi), and the [[Shahadah]].
4. Read a line of Qur'an as part of one of the regular reads of the course of a year
5. Recite the names of Allah in focus and meditation
6. Sit in silence for 15 minutes in meditation
7. Dust down the bed as is the Sunnah.
8. Put on the Qur’an recitation by [Noreen Mohammad Siddiq](app://obsidian.md/noreen-mohammad-siddiq) continuing where you left off this morning and dust down the bed before you get in.
9. Once in bed make [[Dhikr]] until you fall asleep.

## Gratitude for Allah
1. Thank Allah for waking you another day and recite your [[Dua List|morning Du'a for protection]] and greet the recording angels.
2. Note where the Qur'an ended in your tracker and change the audio playing from Qur'an to the [[Adhan]].
3. Fill out your [[Mindset Routine|morning questions]] with a drink of (protein? naturally infused water that healthy for my body) water and brush teeth and weigh yourself with wudu
4. Reciting your [Al-Fatihah](app://obsidian.md/al-fatihah) [Al-Ikhlas](app://obsidian.md/al-ikhlas) , [Al-Falaq](app://obsidian.md/al-falaq), [An-Nas](app://obsidian.md/an-nas), [Ayatul Kursi](app://obsidian.md/ayatul-kursi), and [[Shahadah]]
5. Read a line of Qur'an as part of one of the regular reads of the course of a year
6. Make Tahajuud, Istikarah (based on last nights question), and Witr, and eat Suhoor (with protein drink) before or after.
7. Recite the names of Allah in focus and meditation
8. Sit in silence for 15 minutes in meditation
9. Make your bed
