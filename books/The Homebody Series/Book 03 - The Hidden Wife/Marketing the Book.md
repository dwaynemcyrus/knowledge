## Chapter Summaries
Chapter 1: Introduction This chapter introduces the purpose of the book, which is to guide traditional homebody women towards finding a fulfilling and loving marriage. The author explains who the book is for and provides an overview of the chapters to come. The chapter also includes an explanation of how the book will benefit readers and the worksheets that will be filled out per chapter.

Chapter 2: Accepting Desiring Marriage In this chapter, the author addresses the societal pressure and shame that traditional women may face for wanting marriage. The author encourages readers to embrace the reality that finding a husband takes work and emphasizes the belief in the possibility and acceptance of wanting to be a wife.

Chapter 3: Defining the Man This chapter focuses on understanding what the men that readers desire are looking for in a wife. The author encourages readers to recognize the important qualities that they want in a husband and reminds them that their needs and some of their wants must align with their potential husband for success.

Chapter 4: Being Found By A Man In this chapter, the author provides strategies to help readers be noticed by traditional men who are looking for marriage. The author emphasizes patience and determination while being out in the world networking, as well as building and nurturing a supportive community that wants to see them get married.

Chapter 5: Keeping up the Momentum This chapter discusses the importance of keeping consistent in making oneself findable by men in the world. The author encourages readers to build confidence and self-assurance in the pursuit of a good man and provides advice on dealing with the fears and insecurities that will show up while courting men.

Chapter 6: Conclusion The final chapter provides a recap of the main points discussed in the book, a reflection on the progress made by the reader, and encouragement to continue the journey towards finding a fulfilling and loving marriage. The author also shares final thoughts and words of wisdom and calls readers to action to share their success stories and feedback with the author and community. The conclusion ends with gratitude to the reader for taking the time to read and work through the chapters.
