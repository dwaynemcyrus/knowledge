Fear of facing painful memories and emotions. Fear of the unknown and uncertainty about the outcome of the work. Resistance to change and the discomfort it brings.
### Solution
A **initiation ritual** grounding into the safe, non-judgmental space for initial conversations, offering reassurance and education about the healing process. Gradually introduce the idea of facing emotions and emphasize the manageable steps involved.
