#### Target Audience
**Men Navigating Trauma:** Specifically targeting men seeking to overcome past trauma, with an emphasis on those who are open to integrating both modern therapeutic practices, creative/artistic expression, and spirituality into their healing process and daily lives.


#### Communication Style
- **Empathetic and Inspirational**: Messages that resonate on an emotional level, offering hope and inspiration.
- **Clear and Accessible**: Information presented in a straightforward manner, making it easy for the audience to understand and relate to.
- **Authentic and Personal**: Sharing personal stories and insights to build trust and connection with the audience.
- **Rebellious and Passionate:** Painting a clear picture of the enemy that is being fought against and destroying lives.
