## Job Title: High-End Art Advisor

Company Overview: We are a leading art consultancy firm that specializes in providing bespoke art advisory services to high-net-worth individuals, collectors, museums, and corporate clients globally. Our firm is dedicated to helping our clients build world-class art collections by providing expert advice on buying, selling, and managing art.

Job Overview: We are seeking a highly experienced and knowledgeable art advisor to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a proven track record of working with high-end clients and will have a deep understanding of the art market. The art advisor will be responsible for advising clients on the acquisition and sale of fine art, managing collections, and providing general art market insights and trends.

Responsibilities: • Work closely with clients to understand their art collecting goals and objectives. • Develop and implement strategic plans to build and manage art collections. • Conduct in-depth research on artists, art movements, and market trends to identify investment opportunities. • Source and vet artworks for acquisition, including negotiating prices, managing transactions, and overseeing shipping and installation. • Develop and maintain relationships with galleries, auction houses, and other art world professionals. • Prepare detailed reports and analyses on artworks and collections, including market trends and valuations. • Advise clients on collection management, including conservation, storage, and insurance. • Attend art fairs, exhibitions, and other art world events to stay up-to-date on market developments and opportunities.

Qualifications: • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Art History, Fine Arts, or a related field. • Minimum of 5 years of experience in a high-end art advisory role. • In-depth knowledge of art history, art market trends, and contemporary art. • Strong negotiation and communication skills. • Excellent research and analytical skills. • Demonstrated ability to work collaboratively with clients and other art professionals. • Ability to travel as needed.

This is an exciting opportunity to join a dynamic team of art professionals and work with some of the most prestigious clients in the art world. We offer competitive compensation packages and a supportive work environment that encourages professional development and growth. If you are passionate about art and have a proven track record of success in art advisory, we encourage you to apply.

## Job Title: High-End Gallerist

Company Overview: We are a prominent art gallery specializing in high-end contemporary art, with a reputation for showcasing some of the world's most exciting and innovative artists. Our gallery is committed to providing an exceptional experience for our clients, from first-time art buyers to seasoned collectors, through a dynamic exhibition program and unparalleled client service.

Job Overview: We are seeking an experienced and motivated gallerist to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a proven track record of working with high-end clients and artists, and will have a deep understanding of the contemporary art market. The gallerist will be responsible for overseeing the gallery's exhibition program, managing client relationships, and driving sales and business development.

Responsibilities: 
- Manage the gallery's exhibition program, including selecting and curating artworks, organizing exhibitions, and promoting shows. 
- Develop and maintain relationships with artists, collectors, and other art world professionals. 
- Work closely with clients to understand their art collecting goals and objectives, and provide expert advice on artworks and collecting strategies. 
- Develop and implement sales strategies to drive revenue and meet sales targets. 
- Manage client relationships, including providing exceptional client service, facilitating transactions, and overseeing shipping and installation. • Stay up-to-date on market trends and developments, and attend art fairs, exhibitions, and other art world events. • Work collaboratively with gallery staff to ensure smooth operation of the gallery.

Qualifications: • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Art History, Fine Arts, or a related field. • Minimum of 5 years of experience as a gallerist in a high-end art gallery. • In-depth knowledge of contemporary art, artists, and market trends. • Strong negotiation and communication skills. • Proven ability to build and maintain relationships with clients and artists. • Demonstrated ability to work collaboratively with gallery staff. • Ability to think creatively and strategically, and drive sales and business development.

This is an exciting opportunity to join a dynamic team of art professionals and work with some of the most exciting artists in the contemporary art world. We offer competitive compensation packages and a supportive work environment that encourages professional development and growth. If you are passionate about contemporary art and have a proven track record of success as a gallerist, we encourage you to apply.
