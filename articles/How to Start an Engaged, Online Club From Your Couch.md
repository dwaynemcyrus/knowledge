- Find a communication and coordination website or platform like  [Bookclubz](https://bookclubz.com/) or [Book Movement](https://www.bookmovement.com/)
- Be creative and pick a name for your book club
- Consider beginning with a small size of members to help with scheduling and meeting times
- Decide on a video platform for your meetings
- Pick a book and set a discussion date
- Ask question and share opinions throughout ther eading period to keep people engaged and motivated
- Have a template of discussion question to help make your meetings go smoothly
- Always have another book being voted on or selected before the end of the read
