## How to Begin
- Speaking clearly comes down to reminding yourself to breath to keep oxygen coming to the brain
- Speak slowly to reduce the anxious excitement

## How to Stop Stuttering 
- How I solved this, I decided that I didn’t want to live this way anymore
- Train yourself to breath as this aids in removing the stuttering
- Practice reading out loud by recording and listening to it, hearing yourself allows you to feel more confident in speaking

## Daily Exercises to Speak Better
- Always be hydrated with water at least 3-5 litres of water especially when you first wake up
- “A hydrated mind is a powerful mind”
- Work on your breathing, proper breathing from the stomach in the nose and out the mouth
- Read allowed daily with various volumes and accents
- Speak louder in person to people, to be heard and have people listen

## Your Next Steps 
- get started immediately don’t wait don’t delay
- Read at minimum 1-3 pages and listen to the recording
