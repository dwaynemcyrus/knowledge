### Problem
- Facing the initial discomfort and emotional resistance of confronting the unknown emotional sides (relationship pains, past traumas, confusions about feelings) of his existence (being inadequate emotionally and exposed) with another man who may judge him harshly.
- Managing expectations and understanding that emotional healing is a gradual process that requires patience and perseverance.
### Solution
An informal, educational on-boarding process that front loads access to tools, sets expectations, clarifies desired outcomes, and provides a quick emotional win to reassure the client of his decision to work together. Along with a shared vision for the outcome Marcus wants to create together for himself.
