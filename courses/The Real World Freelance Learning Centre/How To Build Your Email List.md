## Build Your List Easily
- Place your signup into your bio to bring them into your world 
- Give them something for entering your world
- place the signup links at the end of your treads
- Make use of tinyurl.com 
- Keep it simple, don’t focus on fancy software just give value! 
## Collecting Emails
- There’s a variety of email tools
- Give access via the welcome email
- Send weekly emails with insightful stories, that end with promoting your product
## Process Overview 
Twitter link » Landing page with product » Value-driven emails promoting offers

When you are first starting your Twitter do not stress over the email list, you can do this latter when your account has grown.
