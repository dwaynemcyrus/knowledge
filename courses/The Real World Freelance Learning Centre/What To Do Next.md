## BONUS: The Right Way to Approach Clients via Twitter
### Principles to Get Better Results
- Build rapport, like follow, comment
- Lead with value when you enter the DMs
- Create a Loom video as a screen recording auditing them, or adding some form of value
- Do your own research, type these people names and Google them! Don’t kill your rapport by offering something they obviously do not need
- Make it about them, it’s never about you. Help them save time and make money.
- Get to the point! No one wants to read a long DM from people they do not know
- Vary the style of DMs you use until you get more results, Test Test Test!
