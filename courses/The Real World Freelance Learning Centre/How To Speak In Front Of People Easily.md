## How to Speak to Important People 
- Shut up, do not talk, listen 
- Do not try and prove yourself to others ahead of you or who has what you want
- People of wealth do not want to be around people who want to take
- Sit back relax and listen be chill
- Don’t talk about self unless asked and make the response brief and to the point 

## How to Speak in Front of People Easily
- Never forget to focus on your breathing!
- Don’t try to speak too quickly
- Being nervous is natural and okay
- Always give yourself talking points to cover, keep it simple you know your self. Use big spacing and fonts for reference 
- Learn the material you are talking about in advance of you talking about it so you can freestyle off the bullet points
