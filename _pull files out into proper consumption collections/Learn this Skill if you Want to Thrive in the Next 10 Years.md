## The 9 steps to start at online writing business…
1. What’s the problem you are addressing, a topic you cannot shut up about
2. Come up with a novel (unique) perspective, brainstorm the following based on the problem in step one: common problems, benefits for overcoming the problems, common goals associated with the problem (helping people achieve, how to, etc)
3. Write 1000 words on the topic, start with the problem (“lead”, it comes after the headline), provide context on the benefits and the why acting on this problem is important to take action, then step by step advice on how to solve the problem
4. Setup a blog or newsletter platform, long form content is easier to sell with over short form, and this is about building authority. IT’s a good idea to blog your products/services into your newsletter
5. Turn your content into smaller bits of content for X, focused on steps
6. Set your ego aside, and learn about how to get eyes on your profile, people sharing your content is the best way. Network with other accounts that are in your sphere, close online friends to support each other. Comment on big followers content so people check your profile. Pay for influencer marketing. Write your newsletter to be so good people just share and follow.
7. PPP (Pull, Perspective, Punchline) is a system for writing short form content. Copy the structures of other peoples Tweets
8. Create a writing system for yourself to write. Research different frameworks for writing, you need to explore what works for you. Once you have one, share it with others.
9. Branch out to other platforms by using ideas that have been validated from other platforms like X
