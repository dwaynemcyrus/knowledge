### Overview
The "Breakthrough to Boundaries" package is designed to help clients quickly identify and overcome their immediate challenges related to emotional trauma, and unhealthy relationships boundaries, with self and others. Launching the steps into and take the first steps towards personal growth and healthier fulfilling relationships. Each call will be 60 minutes long, providing actionable steps and support materials to facilitate personal growth and resilience.
### Call Breakdown
#### Call 1: Initial Assessment and Understanding Boundaries (Trauma Identification)
**Objective**: Sets a strong foundation by understand the client's background, identify their primary pain points, introduce the concept of boundaries, and set a clear outcome for the end of the coaching sessions.
**Agenda**:
- Introduction and rapport building
- Detailed discussion of the client's history (any trauma's) and current challenges
- Explanation of what boundaries are and their importance
- Identification of any core traumas and their impact
- Outcome setting for the coaching package
**Assignment**: Personal reflection and journaling exercises on current boundaries and boundary violations
[[Philosophy of Integrated Authenticity|Philosophy Fit]]
- **Healing and Safety**: By discussing the client's history and identifying traumas, this call creates a safe space for clients to begin their healing journey.
- **Self-Worth and Empowerment**: Introducing the concept of boundaries helps clients understand their right to protect their well-being, laying the groundwork for empowerment.
#### Call 2: Identifying and Understanding Personal Needs
**Objective**: Help clients identify their personal needs and the areas where boundaries need to be established.
**Agenda**:
- Review of insights from the initial assessment
- Techniques for identifying personal needs and understanding their importance
- Discussion on the impact of unmet needs on emotional well-being
**Assignment**: Create a list of personal needs and areas in life where boundaries are lacking
[[Philosophy of Integrated Authenticity|Philosophy Fit]]
- **Self-Worth and Empowerment**: Recognizing and prioritizing personal needs enhances self-worth and empowers clients to advocate for themselves.
- **Continuous Personal Growth**: Techniques for identifying needs and understanding their importance promote ongoing self-awareness and personal development.
#### Call 3: Communicating Boundaries Effectively
**Objective**: Teach clients how to effectively communicate their boundaries to others.
**Agenda**:
- Discussion on the principles of effective boundary communication
- Practical strategies for articulating and asserting boundaries
- Role-playing scenarios to practice boundary-setting conversations
**Assignment**: Practice boundary-setting conversations in real-life situations and journal reflections on the outcomes
[[Philosophy of Integrated Authenticity|Philosophy Fit]]
- **Intentional Relationships**: Learning to articulate and assert boundaries improves relationship dynamics, fostering healthier and more intentional interactions.
- **Self-Worth and Empowerment**: Effective communication of boundaries reinforces self-worth and empowers clients to maintain their needs and rights in relationships.
#### Call 4: Maintaining Boundaries and Handling Pushback
**Objective**: Develop strategies for maintaining boundaries over time and handling resistance or pushback from others.
**Agenda**:
- Review of progress and key takeaways from previous calls
- Strategies for maintaining boundaries and reinforcing them consistently
- Techniques for dealing with pushback or resistance from others
- Development of a personalized action plan for ongoing boundary maintenance
**Assignment**: Implement the action plan and ongoing self-assessment of boundary practices
