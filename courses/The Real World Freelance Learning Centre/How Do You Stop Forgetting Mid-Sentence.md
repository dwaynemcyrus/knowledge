## How to Stay Calm and Not Be Reactive 
- The way to stay calm is to sit up good posture, hands down, breathe 
- Don’t be reactive take time to think then respond always
- Avoid fidgeting with your hands don’t do this
- Never respond off of instincts unless you have trained your instincts otherwise whatever you say is going to be useless

## How to Stop Forgetting What You Are Saying Mid-Sentence
- Keep yourself breathing 
- When you forget, you keep talking and then ask a question if you can
- Don’t allow yourself to get stuck in the negative feedback loop oh not remembering just keep going through it
- I give this time to be fixed it could take weeks or months but give it time

## How to Have a Deeper Voice
- understanding tonality will give you a deeper voice you have control over it
- breathing, projection, slow pace, speaking from stomach and louder will be better voice
- Stop being timid, feel the power when you speak in your stomach, and speak loudly with command, be calm and responsive
- Speak from your diaphragm
