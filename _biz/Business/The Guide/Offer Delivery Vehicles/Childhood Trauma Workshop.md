### Delivery
1. 

1. We will look into your inner child and discuss it 
2. Tell me about your experiences good and bad and how they shaped your life
3. Look at the resentment or hurt you hold onto with parents 
4. You will go through these writing exercise
5. We will dig deeper into what happened and what control you have now
6. Decide if you need to have a conversation with a parent 
7. If you do we will plan it out by listening all the ways it go wrong and preparing for it, this is boundary preparation 
8. You will schedule a time to have the convo at a time where I will be accessible to support you and debrief 
9. We will debrief from the convo and plan next steps and self-care and recovery 
10. Document on self care and recovery from parental wounds will be provided to address the hurt 
11. In addition a guide on how to prepare and have the convo with parents on challenging subjects
