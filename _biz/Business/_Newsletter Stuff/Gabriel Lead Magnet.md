Lead Magnet: “Foundations of Healing: Essential Boundaries for Thriving Survivors”

Overview

This free eBook/guide would provide a valuable introduction to the Integrated Authenticity philosophy and practical insights into setting boundaries as a key aspect of healing from trauma. It aims to empower survivors by offering actionable steps and fostering a sense of self-worth and safety.

Content Breakdown

Introduction: Welcome to Integrated Authenticity

	•	What is Integrated Authenticity?: Briefly explain the philosophy and its four pillars: Healing and Safety, Self-Worth and Empowerment, Intentional Relationships, and Continuous Personal Growth.
	•	Why Boundaries Matter: Discuss the importance of boundaries in the context of healing and personal growth.

Chapter 1: Understanding Boundaries

	•	What Are Boundaries?: Define boundaries and their types (physical, emotional, mental).
	•	The Role of Boundaries in Healing: Explain how boundaries help in creating a safe space for emotional recovery and growth.

Chapter 2: Identifying Personal Needs and Boundaries

	•	Recognizing Your Needs: Offer exercises and prompts to help Gabriel identify her personal needs and where boundaries are lacking.
	•	Signs of Boundary Violations: Educate on how to recognize when boundaries are being crossed and the impact of such violations.

Chapter 3: Setting and Communicating Boundaries

	•	Steps to Setting Boundaries: Provide a step-by-step guide on how to establish clear and effective boundaries.
	•	Effective Communication Techniques: Tips and scripts for asserting boundaries in a respectful and assertive manner.

Chapter 4: Maintaining Boundaries for Continuous Growth

	•	Dealing with Pushback: Strategies for handling resistance from others when setting boundaries.
	•	Reinforcing Boundaries Over Time: Techniques for maintaining and adjusting boundaries as needed.

Chapter 5: Additional Resources

	•	Worksheets and Exercises: Include downloadable worksheets for personal reflection, boundary-setting practice, and tracking progress.
	•	Further Reading and Support: Recommend books, articles, and support groups that align with the Integrated Authenticity philosophy.

Call to Action

	•	Free Consultation Offer: Invite Gabriel to schedule a free initial consultation call to discuss her journey and how the “Boundaries for Healing” package can support her further.
	•	Join the Community: Encourage her to join a supportive community (like a Facebook group or forum) where she can connect with others on a similar path and receive ongoing support.

Design and Accessibility

	•	Visually Appealing: Use calming colors, engaging graphics, and clear formatting to make the guide easy to read and visually appealing.
	•	Easy to Download: Ensure the eBook is easily accessible via your website or a landing page, with a simple email opt-in form to capture leads.

Summary

This lead magnet will provide Gabriel with valuable insights and practical tools, introducing her to the core principles of Integrated Authenticity while addressing her immediate needs related to boundary setting for healing. By offering this resource, you establish credibility, build trust, and create a pathway for Gabriel to engage further with your coaching services.
