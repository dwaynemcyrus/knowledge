### Give a list of simple activities and strategies to build and maintain self-confidence and self-assurance.
Sure, here are some simple activities and strategies that can help build and maintain self-confidence and self-assurance:

1.  Positive self-talk: Practice talking to yourself in a positive and supportive way. Encourage yourself and acknowledge your strengths and accomplishments.
    
2.  Exercise: Exercise is a great way to boost your mood and self-confidence. Find an exercise routine that you enjoy and make it a part of your daily routine.
    
3.  Practice self-care: Take care of your physical, emotional, and mental well-being. This can include getting enough sleep, eating a healthy diet, and practicing relaxation techniques like meditation or yoga.
    
4.  Set achievable goals: Setting achievable goals and working towards them can help build self-confidence. Start with small goals and celebrate your accomplishments as you achieve them.
    
5.  Learn new skills: Learning new skills and expanding your knowledge can help you feel more confident in yourself and your abilities.
    
6.  Surround yourself with positivity: Surround yourself with positive people who support and encourage you. Avoid negative people and situations that bring you down.
    
7.  Focus on solutions: Instead of dwelling on problems, focus on finding solutions. This can help you feel more in control and confident in your ability to handle challenges.
    
8.  Challenge yourself: Take on new challenges and push yourself out of your comfort zone. This can help you build resilience and confidence in your ability to overcome obstacles.
