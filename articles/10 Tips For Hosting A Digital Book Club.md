Standard book club rules 
- you want to pick a theme or goal for your club and stick with it
- include diverse authors in your reading list
- set a regular schedule
- have [prepared discussion questions](https://www.bustle.com/articles/167822-13-general-book-club-questions-for-any-kind-of-discussion) before every meeting

The Tips
- Make a Facebook page to manage the group
- Make use of Goodreads to give the group a boost online
- Have a set moderator for the discussions, don’t be afraid to vary the communication style
- Make use of Twitter to with an official hashtag to host  a hashtag-bookchat, other chatting tools include Skype, Slack, Fleep, HipChat
- Use online surveys to select the books to read with the club by making use of tools like Google Forms and Survey Monkey
- Make use of your landing page to share more than just meeting dates, share thoughts, opinion, author interviews, book reviews, etc
- Read digital books instead of physical ones to minimize barrier of access
- Hold remote reading sessions
- Consider holding video chats using Zoom, Discord, Google Hangouts
- Setup virtual talks with authors
