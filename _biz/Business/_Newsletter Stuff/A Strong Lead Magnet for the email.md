A strong and life-changing lead magnet that introduces people to your coaching offer and establishes you as a trusted coach could be:

**Title:** "Healing from Within: A Guide to Setting Boundaries and Rediscovering Self-Worth"

**Description:** This lead magnet offers a comprehensive guide that helps trauma survivors and individuals struggling with emotional healing to understand the importance of setting boundaries and rebuilding self-worth. It includes practical exercises, personal stories, and actionable steps to start their journey towards healing and empowerment.

**Key Benefits:**
1. **Practical Guidance:** Provides step-by-step exercises and techniques to begin setting boundaries and improving self-worth.
   
2. **Personal Insights:** Shares personal stories or client testimonials that illustrate the impact of boundary-setting and self-worth on personal growth.

3. **Actionable Steps:** Offers clear, actionable steps that readers can implement immediately to start seeing positive changes in their lives.

4. **Exclusive Content:** Includes exclusive content not found elsewhere, emphasizing the value of signing up for your email list or subscribing to your newsletter.

**Delivery Format:** This lead magnet could be delivered as a downloadable PDF guide, accessible after signing up for your email list. It should be visually appealing, well-organized, and designed to engage readers from the outset.

**Promotion Strategy:** Promote this lead magnet across your social media platforms (like TikTok, X, YouTube) and through your email newsletter. Emphasize the transformative potential of the content and how it aligns with your coaching philosophy of "Boundaries for Connection."

By offering a valuable resource like this, you not only attract potential clients but also demonstrate your expertise and commitment to helping others navigate their healing journey effectively.
