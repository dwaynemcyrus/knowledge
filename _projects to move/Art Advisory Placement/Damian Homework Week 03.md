## Art Advisor Job Description

Full-time Position
$61,000 - $ (+20% Commission on Sales)

We are a leading art consultancy firm that specializes in providing bespoke art advisory services to high-net-worth individuals, emerging collectors, museums, and corporate clients globally. Our firm is dedicated to helping our clients build world-class art collections by providing expert advice on buying, selling, and managing art.

### Responsibilities
- Work closely with clients to understand their art-collecting goals and objectives.
- Develop and implement strategic plans to build and manage art collections.
- Conduct in-depth research on artists, art movements, and market trends to identify investment opportunities.
- Source and vet artworks for acquisition, including negotiating prices, managing transactions, and overseeing shipping and installation.
- Develop and maintain relationships with galleries, auction houses, and other art world professionals.
- Prepare detailed reports and analyses on artworks and collections, including market trends and valuations.
- Advise clients on collection management, including conservation, storage, and insurance.
- Attend art fairs, exhibitions, and other art world events to stay up-to-date on market developments and opportunities.

-   Maintain and develop client relationships through an individual approach, leveraging also on CRM and digital tools.

-   Driving the strategy behind Masterworks Acquisitions
-   Developing and managing relationships with collectors, dealers, galleries and art advisors
-   Sourcing and managing the acquisition of paintings by artists like Lynette Yiadom-Boakye, Keith Haring, and Joan Mitchell
-   Advising on pricing strategy, fair market value, and art market intelligence for blue-chip paintings in the $500k to $5mm price range
-   Driving strategic negotiations for acquisitions of works sourced by the team
-   Proactively generating new business opportunities and deepening existing relationships
-   Vetting prospective sellers
-   Attending art fairs, collector dinners or events, and exhibitions to generate new business
-   Assisting acquisitions team with routine pricing sessions and artist market analysis
-   Viewing paintings and assessing condition prior to acquisition

- Excellent research and analytical skills.
- Demonstrated ability to work collaboratively with clients and other art professionals.
