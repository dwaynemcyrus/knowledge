## Review Checklist
Get Clear
- [ ] Loose paper
- [ ] Digital paper
- [ ] Inbox
- [ ] Empty your head
	- [ ] Trigger List Professional
	- [ ] Trigger List Personal
Get Current
- [ ] Review “Next Actions” List: Check off and fill in any missing next actions to take
- [ ] Review previous calendars data: Review the last week of calendar entries to ensure you have not missed anything important.
- [ ] Review upcoming calendar data: Look 2 weeks ahead and capture any actions, reference material, and items that you may need to prepare for.
- [ ] Review “Waiting For” list: Anything you need to follow up on? Check off what’s already done.
- [ ] Review “Projects” list: Evaluate the status of your projects and desired outcomes. Make sure there is a kick off action for each one in your system. 
- [ ] Review checklists and tracking 
- [ ] Review “Larger Outcomes” list: You want to keep an eye on the bigger picture, the year end and life goals.
- [ ] Review any Reference Material and Cloud Drives.: You want to ensure you are not missing anything! 
Get Creative
- [ ] Review “Someday” list: Removed anything that has been around too long or you have lost interest in. Move out any new projects and actions.
- [ ] Be creative and courageous!: Are there any new ideas and projects you want to get into? Now is the time to get started on them and turn them into reality.
Big Picture Review
- [ ] Questions to ask: Clarify your larger outcomes, long-term goals, visions and principles.
	- [ ] What are your key goals and objectives in your work? 
	- [ ] What should you have in place within a year or three from now?
