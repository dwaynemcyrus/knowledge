## What is Copywriting?
Copywriting is using words to get people to buy things on mass, especially online. 
- Architects of persuasion, emotions, etc
- This is a core skill for success online

This skill allows for the following opportunities 
- Helping existing businesses make more money
- Launching your own business 
- Selling your own products online

This is about active income NOT passive income, you need to work and produce results for people to sell.

## How to Accelerate you Ability to Learn and Work
- Copywriting is your ability to think persuasively and this ability is a muscle
- You need to train your body and brain to the point of failure 
- In most cases when you reach the failure point (the bleeding is edge) we reach for our phones and you need to practice pushing yourself beyond this to expand your creative thinking
- Binaural beats can offer focus when working, it may also just be a placebo effect, here's [an example](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mg7netw1JuM).

Steps to Deep Work:
- This is to be achieved by doing [[deep work]], you must be setup to be completely emersed in what you are doing. Keeping your phone completely out of sight is key. 
- Set a timer for 15-20 minutes after the first 10 or so you will enter flow where you can generate meaningful work
- You should be able to hit 90-120 minutes after training this week after this hits diminishing returns at that point you need to rest by doing something different like play guitar, go for a walk, read, etc.

Tips
- Drink 2 gallons of water a day to keep your brain functioning 
- Get your blood pumping during your breaks, the more O2 the better for your body
- Deep Work is a superpower this where you will be able to out perform others

Notes to self
- You have a built in Deep Work ability, this is a natural state for you 
- Read the book [[deep-work-by-cal-newport|Deep Work]] to learn more about this, so you can fine tune yourself
