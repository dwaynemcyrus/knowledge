## Time for Threads
### First Tweet
- Lead with a hook
- share an interesting story
- Show how the reqder can achieve the same great results 
- Attach an image this increases engagement 
- End with “THREAD” or “1/“
### Second Tweet
- Insert a CTA such as “before we get started, retweet the first tweet”
### Next Tweets
- Fill with value
- have each sentence lead to the next
- Keep filler words out and do not allow it to get boring
- Use proper spacing
- To keep people reading you always end with anticipation 
### Last Tweet
- Insert the CTA again, it could be a gift, ask to retweet, etc.
## Additional Info
- Aim for 1-2 threads per week
- People don’t give unless you ask, ASK FOR WHAT YOU WANT!
- Threads are meant to break down a topic you are knowledgeable about to build your authority in your niche
- Threads are good for giving lessons from your own life, this makes you NOT boring
